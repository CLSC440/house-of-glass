User Data Deletion Instructions

If you want House Of Glass to delete personal data associated with your website account or your interaction with our connected channels, follow the steps below.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to hady@hg-alshour.online.
  2. Use the subject line: Data Deletion Request.
  3. Include your full name, phone number, email address, username, or any conversation details that help us identify your data.
  4. We will review the request and confirm deletion or required clarification within a reasonable time.

Delete From Your Account

If you have a website account and the account deletion option is available in your account settings, you may also request deletion by signing in and using that option directly.

What Will Be Deleted

Upon successful verification, we will delete or anonymize eligible personal information associated with your website account, profile details, saved favorites, inquiries, or connected Meta interaction, except where retention is required for legal, accounting, or security purposes.

Contact

For any deletion-related request, contact: hady@hg-alshour.online.
